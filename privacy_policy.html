Privacy Policy

App: Simmang Tech
Developer: Simmang Tech
Last updated: March 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Simmang Tech ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Simmang Tech mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your name and email address (provided by Google Sign-In)
  • A unique user ID for your account

Device & Usage Data

To provide the smart home control service, we collect:

  • Device identifiers for your registered Simmang smart devices
  • Device status, socket states, and schedule configurations you set
  • Timestamps of device activity (e.g., last seen online)

Bluetooth (BLE) Data

The App uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) only during the initial device setup to send Wi-Fi credentials to your Simmang device. We do not use Bluetooth to track your location. No Bluetooth data is stored on our servers.

Network Information

During device pairing, the App reads the name of your connected Wi-Fi network (SSID) to pre-fill the pairing form. This information is sent directly to your device over Bluetooth and is not stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account
  • To display and control your registered smart devices
  • To sync device schedules and settings across sessions
  • To show real-time device status in the app

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your account and device data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication and Firebase Realtime Database), hosted on Google's infrastructure. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Google Firebase — for authentication and data storage (Google's Privacy Policy applies)
  • Google Sign-In — for account login
  • When required by law or to protect user safety

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data

To exercise these rights, contact us at: appsimmangtech@gmail.com

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
